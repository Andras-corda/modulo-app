Privacy Policy

Modulo is built with privacy as a top priority.

Last updated: April 2025

Summary

Modulo collects no personal data. All your data is stored exclusively on your machine. Nothing leaves your computer.

Data collected by the app

The Modulo application collects nothing. It sends no data to any server. It contains no trackers, no analytics, no telemetry.

Cookies

This site uses a single functional cookie (modulo_rgpd) to remember your banner preference. It expires after 365 days.

Open source

Modulo is fully open source. You can audit the code at GitHub.
